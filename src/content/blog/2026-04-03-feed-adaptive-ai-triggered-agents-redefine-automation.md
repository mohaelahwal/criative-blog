Adaptive AI has unveiled an innovative solution with their new "Triggered Agents," poised to redefine business automation and efficiency. These AI agents are engineered to take proactive, autonomous action in direct response to specific real-time events across various platforms. A key application highlighted is their capability to manage customer service around the clock, adeptly reading emails, diagnosing and solving customer issues, and formulating automatic replies independently, thereby ensuring continuous support without manual intervention.

Beyond enhancing customer service, Triggered Agents demonstrate remarkable versatility across diverse operational domains. Illustrative examples include an agent automatically finding a supplier and drafting a purchase order when Shopify stock levels are low, or initiating recovery protocols after reviewing transaction history if a Stripe payment fails. In development workflows, an agent can review GitHub Pull Requests, analyze code differences, and flag potential risks. Adaptive AI is inviting users to experience this technology firsthand with a free trial, and offers $50 in credits for those who share a compelling use case.
