Pika Labs has just unveiled a groundbreaking beta feature that significantly elevates human-AI interaction: a real-time video chat skill for AI agents. Leveraging their new PikaStream1.0 model, this innovation allows AI agents to engage in live video calls, complete with a virtual 'face' and 'voice.' This development is poised to make digital conversations with AI far more natural and immersive, bridging a critical gap in current AI communication paradigms.

Beyond mere visual and auditory presence, PikaStream1.0 ensures robust functionality. The skill meticulously preserves an agent's established memory and personality, fostering continuity in discussions. Crucially, it enables real-time adaptability during calls. When deployed with a Pika AI Self, agents gain the remarkable ability to execute complex, agentic tasks directly within the video conference, marking a significant step towards truly collaborative and functional AI partners in a virtual setting.
