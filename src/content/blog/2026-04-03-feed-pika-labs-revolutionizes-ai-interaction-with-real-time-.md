Pika Labs is pushing the boundaries of AI interaction with the recent release of a beta video chat skill for AI agents. Powered by their innovative new real-time model, PikaStream1.0, this groundbreaking feature allows users to engage in video calls with multiple AI agents simultaneously. The platform is designed to facilitate dynamic, real-time problem-solving and collaborative work, transforming how we interact with artificial intelligence and enabling agents to respond and assist in truly interactive scenarios.

This advanced skill offers significant enhancements to the agent experience, preserving an agent's memory and personality throughout interactions. Crucially, it provides real-time adaptability, ensuring that agents can respond fluidly to evolving conversations and tasks. When integrated with a user's Pika AI Self, agents gain the ability to execute complex agentic tasks directly during the video call, marking a significant leap forward in practical, real-time AI utility and paving the way for more intuitive and effective digital assistance.
