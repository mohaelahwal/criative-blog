According to recent observations from el.cine (@EHuanglu), Seedance 2.0 is making significant waves in the 3D animation landscape. The platform is being lauded for its robust capabilities, particularly in rendering animated characters with remarkable effectiveness. This marks a notable advancement for animators seeking powerful tools to bring their creative visions to life with enhanced efficiency and visual fidelity.

While Seedance 2.0 demonstrates exceptional performance with stylized or animated figures, el.cine also highlighted a specific area for future refinement: the generation of realistic human faces. It appears that outputs involving real human faces still frequently encounter 'flagging' issues, suggesting an ongoing challenge in achieving seamless integration or accurate representation in this particular domain. Despite this, its overall impact on 3D animation workflows remains highly promising for character-focused projects.
